I am a Cloud & DevOps Engineer with 2.1+ years of hands-on experience in AWS environments, infrastructure automation, and production support. My expertise spans Infrastructure as Code using Terraform, modern CI/CD pipelines in GitLab, containerization with Docker, and robust Linux & Shell scripting automation.

I am deeply experienced with AWS Serverless architectures, regularly leveraging Lambda and Step Functions alongside RDS, as well as CloudWatch for extensive monitoring. Handling high-availability system management and incident RCA in Agile environments has shaped my approach: I build resilient systems that scale gracefully while empowering teams through seamless deployments.

In the quiet hours, you'll find me exploring the boundaries of distributed systems and hardening CI/CD secrets management. I believe that DevOps is not just a role, but a culture of radical transparency and structural integrity.
